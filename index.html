About Our Play School

At Little Explorers, we believe that early childhood is a magical time of discovery. Our play-based curriculum is designed to nurture each child's natural curiosity and foster a lifelong love of learning.

Our experienced educators create a safe, stimulating environment where children can explore, create, and build the foundational skills they need for future success.

Our Programs

We offer age-appropriate programs designed to meet the developmental needs of every child.

Toddlers (2-3 years)

Our toddler program focuses on sensory exploration, early social skills, and developing independence through guided play.

Preschool (3-4 years)

Preschoolers engage in more structured activities that build pre-academic skills while continuing to emphasize creative play.

Pre-K (4-5 years)

Our Pre-K program prepares children for kindergarten with a balanced approach of play-based learning and academic readiness.
